ExpenseTracker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 March 2026 (updated for advertising disclosure)  ·  Developer: OffSecIntel  ·  Platform: Android

1. Introduction

ExpenseTracker ("the App") is an Android application developed by OffSecIntel, a proprietorship. The App helps you log and categorise personal financial transactions, track budgets, and sync your data to your own Google Drive spreadsheet.

This Privacy Policy describes what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Collect

  • Google account email address — collected during Google Sign-In solely to identify your Google Drive and Sheets resources. No other Google profile data is accessed.
  • Google Drive / Sheets access — the App requests write access to create and update a Google Spreadsheet ("Updo Expenses") in your Drive. This spreadsheet is owned by you; OffSecIntel has no access to it.
  • Incoming SMS messages (on-device only) — the App listens for incoming SMS to detect banking and UPI transaction notifications. SMS content is parsed in real-time on your device and is never transmitted off-device, stored in any OffSecIntel server, or logged anywhere outside the App.
  • Transaction data you enter — amounts, categories, payment methods, dates, and remarks. This data is stored locally on your device (encrypted Hive database) and optionally synced to your own Google Spreadsheet.
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob) — the App displays non-intrusive advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), IP address, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy; OffSecIntel does not receive or store it.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

Your financial data — transactions, SMS content, bank details — is never used for advertising or shared with any ad network.
  • No SMS content is ever transmitted off your device.
  • No full bank account numbers, card numbers, CVV codes, or PINs are collected or stored.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No transaction data, account balances, or financial history is shared with advertisers.
  • No data is sent to any OffSecIntel server — the only external endpoints are Google APIs.
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included in the App.

4. How Data Is Used

  • Google account email — used to authenticate with Google OAuth 2.0 and to identify the correct Google Drive for sync. Never used for marketing or shared with any third party.
  • Google Drive sync — your transaction records are written to a spreadsheet in your Google Drive. OffSecIntel has no access to this spreadsheet.
  • SMS parsing — incoming messages from known bank/UPI senders are parsed on-device to extract transaction amounts and merchants. Parsed data pre-fills the expense entry form for your review; you confirm or discard each suggested entry. No raw SMS text leaves your device.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage — all transaction data is stored on your device using Hive, an encrypted key-value database. Data is protected by Android's app sandboxing; other apps cannot access it.
  • Cloud storage — synced data resides in your own Google Drive spreadsheet, governed by Google's security and privacy controls. OffSecIntel does not operate or access any cloud server containing your data.
  • OAuth tokens — Google access and refresh tokens are stored securely by the Google Sign-In SDK on your device and are never transmitted to OffSecIntel.

6. Permissions Explained

INTERNET Required for Google Sign-In, Google Drive sync, and serving advertisements via Google AdMob.
RECEIVE_SMS Allows the App to be notified of incoming SMS messages in real-time for automatic transaction detection. READ_SMS is intentionally NOT requested — the App cannot access your SMS inbox or historical messages; it only receives new messages as they arrive. This permission is Play Store policy compliant.

8. Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data (transactions, settings, cached tokens) from your device.
  • Google Drive data — your expense spreadsheet remains in your Google Drive after uninstallation. You can delete it at any time from drive.google.com.
  • Google account access — you can revoke the App's OAuth permissions at any time from myaccount.google.com/permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
